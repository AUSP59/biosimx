# Benchmarking Policy
- Benchmarks must pin CPU governor and disable turbo where possible.
- Use dedicated machines or containers; avoid noisy neighbors.
- Report medians and p95; include hardware/OS/compiler details.
- Keep benchmark inputs and scripts versioned under `bench/` or `tools/bench/`.
